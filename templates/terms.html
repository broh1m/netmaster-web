Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use NetMaster.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password (securely hashed).
  • Notes & Calculation Results: Any notes or saved subnet calculations you create.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as page visits, feature usage, and device/browser info.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the NetMaster service.
  • To allow you to create, view, and manage notes and calculation results.
  • To communicate important updates or security notices.
  • To analyze usage trends and improve user experience.

3. User Account & Authentication

  • Account creation is required for saving notes and results.
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • You can update or delete your account at any time by contacting support.

4. Data Retention

  • Your account data, notes, and saved results are retained as long as your account is active.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

5. Cookies & Analytics

  • We use cookies for authentication and session management.
  • Basic analytics may be collected to help us improve the service. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising.

6. Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • You may request a copy of your data or ask us to remove it at any time.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at support@netmaster.com.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Please check this page for the latest information.
